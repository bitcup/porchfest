Performers

Winchester Porchfest needs performers. Sign up now to be a performer!

Winchester Porchfest
Saturday, June 12, 2021 (rain date June 13)
12noon – 5:30pm

What type of performers is Porchfest looking for?
  • We welcome performances of all types: Music, Dance, Poetry, Storytelling.
  • We seek solo acts, duets, small bands, large bands, dance groups, poets and story tellers.
  • All ages welcome, preference given to Winchester-affiliated groups.
Where do I perform?
  • We match you to a host in Winchester.
  • We will try to accept all entries and all specific requests for venues, space permitting.
  • You’ll have a one-hour time slot between 12noon – 5:30pm.
What time slots are available for performing?

We are looking for performances during the following slots:

What do I need to bring as a performer?
  • You provide your own sound equipment, if you require one.
  • You may sell your merch (if you’ve got it).
How do I sign up to be a Porchfest performer?
  • Fill out the form below to sign up.
  • If you have questions, please use our contact form to ask questions (we’ll get right back to you).
  • Deadline to sign up as a performer: June 5, 2021.

We hope you have a great time at Porchfest. By agreeing to perform, you also:

  • Agree to participate at your own risk.
  • Agree to hold performers, hosts, volunteers and/or organizers free of any claim for damages in the event of accident, injury or disappointment.
  • Agree to allow Winchester Porchfest to use photos, sound and/or videos of your portion of the event to publicize future Porchfests.
  • Agree to follow the Massachusetts COVID guidelines in place on the day of Porchfest.

Note: A person under 18 can perform at this event only if he/she/they have permission from their legal guardian.
