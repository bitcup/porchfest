Performers

Winchester Porchfest
Saturday, June 10, 2023 (rain date June 11)
12noon – 5:30pm

What type of performers is Porchfest looking for?
  • We welcome performances of all types: Music, Dance, Poetry, Storytelling.
  • We seek solo acts, duets, small bands, large bands, dance groups, poets and story tellers.
  • All ages welcome, preference given to Winchester-affiliated groups.
Where do I perform?
  • We match you to a host in Winchester.
  • If you have a preferred host site for your performance, you can indicate so in the form below. Also, please have your preferred site sign up as a Porchfest host.
  • If you want to perform at your own site, please make sure to sign up as both a host and performer.
  • We will try to accept all entries and all specific requests for venues, space permitting.
  • You’ll have a one-hour time slot between 12noon – 5:30pm.

  • You provide your own sound equipment, if you require one.
  • You may sell your merch (if you’ve got it).
