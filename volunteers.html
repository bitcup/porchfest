Join our grassroots volunteer committee to help us plan for the event, or just help out on the day of the event. We’d love to have you!

Deadline to sign up as a volunteer: May 15, 2021.

We hope you have a great time at Porchfest. By agreeing to volunteer, you also agree to participate at your own risk. You agree to hold performers, hosts, volunteers and/or organizers free of any claim for damages in the event of accident, injury or disappointment. You agree to allow Winchester Porchfest to use photos, sound and/or videos of your portion of the event to publicize future Porchfests. A person under 18 can volunteer at this event only if he/she/they have permission from their legal guardian.
