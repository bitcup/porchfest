Volunteers

Please visit our SignUpGenius to sign up for Porchfest 2024 volunteer opportunities.

Winchester Porchfest
Saturday, June 8, 2024 (rain date June 9)
12noon – 5:30pm
6:00pm - 7:30pm Finale in Town Common

We are looking for help in several areas, and we encourage you to signup for the areas in which you can contribute. You get to listen to great performances while make Porchfest a success -- we’d love to have you!

We hope you have a great time at Porchfest. By agreeing to volunteer, you also:

  • Agree to participate at your own risk.
  • Agree to hold performers, hosts, volunteers and/or organizers free of any claim for damages in the event of accident, injury or disappointment.
  • Agree to allow Winchester Porchfest to use photos, sound and/or videos of your portion of the event to publicize future Porchfests.

Note: A person under 18 can volunteer at this event only if he/she/they have permission from their legal guardian.
