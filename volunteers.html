Volunteers

Winchester Porchfest needs your help on the day of the event. We are looking for volunteers to act as porch monitors.
You get to listen to great, free music while helping the community hatch out of the pandemic. Sign up now to be a porch monitor and help make the first Winchester Porchfest a success -- we’d love to have you!

Winchester Porchfest
Saturday, June 12, 2021 (rain date June 13)
12noon – 5:30pm

Download the Porchfest Monitor Instructions Sheet (PDF)

We hope you have a great time at Porchfest. By agreeing to volunteer, you also:

  • Agree to participate at your own risk.
  • Agree to hold performers, hosts, volunteers and/or organizers free of any claim for damages in the event of accident, injury or disappointment.
  • Agree to allow Winchester Porchfest to use photos, sound and/or videos of your portion of the event to publicize future Porchfests.
  • Agree to follow the Massachusetts COVID guidelines in place on the day of Porchfest.

Note: A person under 18 can volunteer at this event only if he/she/they have permission from their legal guardian.
