Hosts

Winchester Porchfest needs porches. Sign up now to be a host!

Winchester Porchfest
Saturday, June 12, 2021 (rain date June 13)
12noon – 6pm

What does a Porchfest host do?
  • You will host performers from 12 – 5pm (one performance per hour).
  • The Porchfest committee will match up performers to your porch.
What does a Porchfest host provide?
  • Performance site - a porch or patio, a driveway or parking lot, with room for performers and their equipment
  • Area for an audience - just make sure it won’t block sidewalks or driveways
  • Day-of coordination - you will be the performers go-to person, making sure acts end on time and handling any issues that come up
  • Use of your own trash and recycling bins and handling of disposal afterwards
  • Keeping your neighbors in the loop about the day's events - please be a good neighbor!
  • Adherence to the state's COVID guidelines.
What does the Porchfest team provide for hosts?
  • The Porchfest team will provide disinfectant wipes and hand sanitizer to hosts.
How do I sign up to be a Porchfest host?
  • Fill out the form below to sign up.
  • If you have questions, please use our contact form to ask questions (we’ll get right back to you).
  • Deadline to sign up as a host: May 15, 2021.

By filling out the form below, you are agreeing that you will provide the following:

  • A suitable performance site – The site must be capable of holding a group of musicians and their equipment, in a location that is viewable by a small audience.
  • A suitable viewing area – The area should comfortably fit about 20-30 people, including some space for chairs for those who want or need them. The area MUST NOT block or impede traffic, or encroach on neighbors property without their permission.
  • Receptacles for trash and recycling, and accept responsibility for cleaning the area around their site at day’s end.
  • Coordination of all logistical issues with the performers and the audience at their site, including making sure acts end on time and that they are not too loud for the surrounding environment, etc. In most cases, the performers or the event organizers will have helped pre-organize the day, but venue hosts may be the go-to persons on the day of the event.
  • Responsibility for letting your neighbors know about the day’s events and ensuring that neighbors are kept "in the loop".

We hope you have a great time at Porchfest. By agreeing to host, you also agree to participate at your own risk. You agree to hold performers, hosts, volunteers and/or organizers free of any claim for damages in the event of accident, injury or disappointment. You agree to allow Winchester Porchfest to use photos, sound and/or videos of your portion of the event to publicize future Porchfests.
