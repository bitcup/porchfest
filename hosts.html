What does a Porchfest host provide/do?
  • Performance site. A porch or patio, a driveway or a parking lot, with room for performers and their equipment.
  • Area for an audience. Make sure the audience area does not block sidewalks or driveways.
  • Coordination on Porchfest day. You will be the performer's go-to person, making sure acts end on time and handling any issues that come up.
  • Cleanup. You are responsible for cleanup after performances, using your own trash and recycling bins.
  • Keeping your neighbors in the loop. Please be a good neighbor!
  • Displaying a Porchfest lawn sign at your site. Also, please tell your friends about the event.
What does the Porchfest team provide for hosts?

The Porchfest team will:

  • Coordinate signups, matching performers with hosts, logistics.
  • Help in assessing and planning the layout of the venue if necessary.
  • Provide lawn signs.
  • List your location and performers on our website and in our program.
