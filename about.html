About

Winchester Porch Fest

Welcome to the FIRST ever Winchester Porch Fest!

The festival is a one day volunteer-driven free community event that provides public venues for individuals and groups to share their music and performance art. Our goal is for venues around downtown Winchester to become more festive, promote walking, and encourage patronage of the downtown businesses. We hope to attract many art-related activities, including:

  • Music of any kind (rock, jazz, hip hop/rap, folk, classical, blue grass, Americana, kids music, international, choral, opera, etc.)
  • Poetry
  • Dance
  • Story telling

The venues will include porches, driveways, parking lots, etc. Participation is open to all ages, with preference given to groups that have Winchester residents in them.

We hope to finish the day with a Grand Finale on the Winchester Common.

Where

Downtown Winchester, MA

When

Saturday June 13, 2020 starting at 1:30pm
