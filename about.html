About

Welcome to the FIRST ever Winchester Porchfest!

Our festival is a one day, volunteer-driven, free community event that provides public venues for individuals and groups to share their music and performance art. Our goal is to cluster venues around downtown to promote a more festive environment, promote walking, and encourage patronage of the downtown businesses. We hope to attract many performance-related activities, including:

  • Music of any kind (rock, jazz, hip hop/rap, folk, classical, blue grass, Americana, kids music, international, choral, opera, etc.)
  • Poetry
  • Dance
  • Story telling

The venues will include porches, driveways, parking lots, etc. Participation is open to all ages, with preference given to groups that have Winchester residents in them.

We hope to finish the day with a Grand Finale on the Winchester Common.
