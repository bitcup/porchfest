Join us for a festive day of music and performance all around Winchester.

Winchester Porchfest seeks performers, venues (porches and patios, driveways and parking lots), and we welcome everyone who wants to celebrate music. Volunteer to help make it happen, or spend the day being a great audience with your family and friends.

You’ll see:

  • Music of all kinds
  • Poetry
  • Dance
  • Story telling

Sign up as a performer, host, or volunteer.

Open to all ages, and preference given to Winchester-affiliated groups.

Porchfest is organized and run by local volunteers and is funded in part by a grant from the Winchester Cultural Council.
